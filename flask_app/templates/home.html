NFL Schedule App

Welcome to the NFL Scheduling App! This platform presents a prototype schedule for the 2025 NFL season, created to maximize primetime television viewership while respecting league constraints.

Please explore the three sections of the app to learn more about the schedule!

1

League Schedule

View the complete schedule grid for all 32 teams across 18 weeks.

2

Team Analysis

Dive deeper into individual team schedules and viewership projections

3

Analysis

Explore the methodology and optimization techniques used to create this schedule.

How Does It Work?

1

Data Collection

Gather information from web sources about primetime television viewership and 2025 NFL schedule parameters.

2

Viewership Modeling

Use historical data to determine variables that drive viewership and project audience numbers.

3

Scheduling

Apply mathematical optimization to find the optimal schedule respecting all constraints.

4

App Creation

Build this interactive web application to visualize and explore the results.

Limitations

This schedule is a prototype and has several limitations compared to a real NFL schedule:

  • Limited viewership data (only 2 seasons, 2022-23)
  • Restricted to traditional primetime windows
  • Limited computational power (personal laptop, free solver)
  • Missing constraints (international games, stadium conflicts, bye week restrictions)
